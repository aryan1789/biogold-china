# Bio Gold China: Chinese Copy Rules

This file governs all Chinese (简体中文) copy written for the Bio Gold China site.
Apply these rules to every string in zh.json, product JSONs, and any Chinese text in page components.

---

## Brand Name

Always write the brand as **Bio Gold** (English, two words, space between).
Never translate or transliterate. Foreign brands in China keep their English names.

---

## Two Registers

### Consumer-facing (homepage hero, product benefits, product pages)
Conversational. Direct. Warm but not gushing.
- Use 你 not 您
- Short sentences. One idea at a time.
- Lead with the feeling or outcome, not the ingredient
- Plain vernacular Mandarin. No classical Chinese feel.
- OK to use 还、也、就、其实、其实

### B2B / Professional (partner page, regulatory sections, research page)
Professional but human. Not bureaucratic.
- 您 is acceptable for distributor-facing copy
- Clear structure. One point per sentence.
- No stiff official language (官方腔)
- Still warm. Never cold or transactional.

---

## What NOT to Write (Chinese AI Tells)

These are the Chinese equivalents of the English banned patterns. Every one is a tell that AI wrote it.

1. **赋能** (empower). Banned. Overused in Chinese corporate copy.
2. **打造** (craft/build). Banned. Everyone "打造" everything. Use 做、建、推出 instead.
3. **助力** (empower/help). Banned. Use 帮、支持 instead.
4. **引领** (lead/pioneer). Banned. Use 领先、走在前面 instead.
5. **颠覆** (disrupt/revolutionise). Banned. Too dramatic, too overused.
6. **生态** used metaphorically (ecosystem). Banned unless literally about forests.
7. **赋予** (bestow/grant). Banned. Sounds stiff and archaic.
8. **彰显** (embody/showcase). Banned. Corporate cliché.
9. **破折号** (——). No em dashes. Use comma 、period 。or line break instead.
10. **省略号滥用** (overusing ……). Use sparingly. Not as a vague trailing thought.
11. **堆砌形容词** (adjective stacking). No 高端、精致、卓越、领先 in the same sentence.
12. **"无论你是...还是..."** (whether you're X or Y). Banned fake-inclusive opener.
13. **感叹号滥用** (too many exclamation marks). Max one per section. Zero is fine.
14. **"亲爱的"** as an opener. Not used in modern brand copy.
15. **过度谦虚** (false modesty phrases). No "小小产品"、"微薄之力".
16. **冗余叠字** (redundant repetition). 非常非常好 = bad. 很好 = fine.

## What TO Write

- **Short sentences.** 两句话能说清的，不要用三句。
- **Plain words.** 能用简单字的不用复杂字。用 用 not 使用. 用 做 not 进行.
- **Direct.** 好产品不需要过度解释。说清楚就行。
- **Varied rhythm.** Short punch. Then a sentence that explains and breathes. Then another short one.
- **Warm but understated.** 真诚不是靠感叹号，是靠说到点上。

## Key Vocabulary Guide

| English | Use this | Not this |
|---|---|---|
| supports / maintains | 支持、有助于 | 赋能、助力 |
| energy | 活力、精力 | 能量爆发 (too dramatic) |
| natural | 天然 | 纯天然 (overused) |
| New Zealand | 新西兰 | 纽西兰 (less common in mainland) |
| forest | 森林 | 丛林 |
| science / research | 科研、研究 | 尖端科技 |
| premium / quality | 优质、品质 | 高端、顶级 (overused) |
| distributor | 经销商 | 代理商 (both OK, 经销商 preferred) |
| partner | 合作伙伴 | 伙伴 |
| sublingual | 舌下 | 口含 |
| bioactive | 生物活性 | 生物活性成分 (first use only, then abbreviate) |
| harvest | 采收 | 采集 |
| South Island NZ | 新西兰南岛 | 新西兰南部 |
| provenance | 产地溯源、原产地 | 出处 |

## Sentence Ending Guidance

- Marketing copy: end with 。 Avoid ！ unless genuinely surprising.
- Instructions/steps: end with 。
- B2B: end with 。 Professional, period.
- Never end with ～ or ellipsis ……unless there's a specific stylistic reason.

---

## Numbers and Units

- Keep Arabic numerals: 25,000, 40%, 2分钟, 4年
- Write units in Chinese: 分钟 not min, 年 not yr
- Dates: 月/年 format

## Self-Audit Before Saving

- Any em dashes (——)? Replace with comma or period.
- Any stacked adjectives (高端精致卓越)? Strip to one.
- Any banned corporate vocab (赋能、打造、助力、颠覆)? Rewrite.
- Any exclamation marks? Check if genuinely needed.
- Reads like a person wrote it, not a marketing bot?
- Consumer copy uses 你? B2B copy uses 您?
- Sentences varied in length?
